Our Nursery Programs

Age Groups & Classes

We offer classes for children aged 3 to 5 years, with programs designed to meet the developmental needs of each age group.

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum focuses on play-based learning, early literacy, social skills, and creative arts, ensuring a well-rounded foundation for all children.

Learning Areas

We emphasize key developmental areas, including Language Development, Cognitive Skills, Motor Skills, Socialization, and Emotional Growth.

Daily Activities

Children engage in activities such as storytime, arts and crafts, outdoor play, and sensory exploration to develop their creativity and motor skills.

Teacher Qualifications

Our teachers are certified in early childhood education and have extensive experience working with young children to nurture their growth and learning.

Parent Involvement

We welcome parents to actively participate in classroom activities and special events, fostering a strong home-school connection.

Learning Through Play

We believe that children learn best through hands-on, interactive play, which nurtures creativity, curiosity, and problem-solving skills.

Safety and Security

Your child's safety is our priority. Our school is equipped with secure entry systems and a safe, child-friendly environment.

Facilities and Resources

Our school features a spacious outdoor play area, a dedicated art studio, and sensory areas to support children's development in a creative setting.

Special Programs

We host education tours ,swimming ,ballet dance ,cultural day, graduations and concerts to expand learning beyond the classroom, providing children with real-world experiences.
