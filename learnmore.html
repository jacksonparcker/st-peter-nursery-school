About St. Peter's Church of Uganda Nursery School

At St. Peter's Nursery School, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and educational environment where pupils can thrive in their early years. Our curriculum is designed to develop the intellectual, emotional, and social skills of young learners, setting them up for success in their academic journey and in life.

Our Mission

To instil discipline and quality education.

Our Curriculum

We offer a rich curriculum that includes the following core areas:

  • Language Development: Building early reading, writing, and speaking skills.
  • Learning Area3: Health habits and social habits.
  • Learning area 4: Introducing basic number concepts in a fun and engaging way.
  • Creative Arts: Encouraging imagination through art, singing, and dramatic play.
  • Physical Education: Promoting physical activity and healthy habits.
  • Social and Emotional Development: Fostering empathy, collaboration, and resilience.
