About Our School

Learn more about our mission, values, and commitment to quality education.

Introduction to the School

The school begun in 1993, And was founded by ST Peters church of Uganda Community.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower every student to achieve their highest potential by providing high-quality, accessible education in a nurturing and inclusive environment. We aim to cultivate a love of learning that will last a lifetime.

Vision Statement

We aspire to be a leading institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and the holistic development of students. We envision a future where our graduates contribute meaningfully to society and lead with integrity and confidence.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest ethical standards in all aspects of life.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards of academic achievement.
  • Inclusivity: Ensuring a welcoming and respectful environment for all.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and forward-thinking in education.
  • Collaboration: Fostering teamwork and mutual respect within the community.
  • Responsibility: Instilling a sense of accountability in all our students.

History of the School

Our school was founded in 2000 with the goal of providing quality education to children in our community. Over the years, we've expanded our programs and facilities, adding a state-of-the-art library, sports complex, and advanced science labs.

Achievements & Accolades

Our school has earned recognition for academic excellence, having received several awards in national competitions. We've also received accolades for our sports teams and extracurricular programs.

Faculty & Leadership Team

School Facilities

We offer a wide range of facilities including spacious classrooms, a modern sports complex, a well-equipped library, and science labs that encourage hands-on learning. Our campus is designed to foster both academic and personal growth.

Student Life
